Jacqueline's Famous Vegan Chili

I've made this chili so many times. It's literally perfect and very nutritious. Give it a go.

Ingredients:

  • Black Beans
  • Red Kidney Beans
  • Red Bell Pepper
  • Yellow Bell Pepper
  • Jalapeno
  • Yellow Onion
  • Taco Seasoning

Directions:

  1. Get your ingredients
  2. Throw em in a pot
  3. Let it simmer
  4. Stir it up
  5. Gobble gobble and enjoy
I hope you enjoy this delicious chili!
